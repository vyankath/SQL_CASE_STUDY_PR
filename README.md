## Introduction
Danny's Pizza Runner is a new business that aims to change pizza delivery by offering an Uber-like service. As Danny starts this venture, data is key to understanding customer preferences, improving operations, and growing the business.
---
## Datasets
Danny has provided several important datasets for analyzing Pizza Runner's operations:
1. **Runners**: Information about runners, including their registration dates.
2. **Customer Orders**: Details about each customer order, including pizza types, exclusions, extras, and order times.
3. **Runner Orders**: Records of orders assigned to runners, including pickup times, distances, durations, and cancellations.
4. **Pizza Names**: Information on available pizza types.
5. **Pizza Recipes**: Standard toppings for each pizza type.
6. **Pizza Toppings**: A list of toppings with their IDs and names.

## Case Study Questions
### A. Pizza Metrics
- How many pizzas were ordered?
- How many unique customer orders were made?
- What was the highest number of pizzas delivered in a single order?
- How many pizzas were ordered each hour of the day?
### B. Runner and Customer Experience
- What was the average time it took for each runner to pick up an order from the Pizza Runner HQ?
- What was the average distance traveled for each customer?
- What was the difference between the longest and shortest delivery times for all orders?
### C. Ingredient Optimization
- What are the standard ingredients for each pizza?
- What was the most commonly added extra?
- Generate an order item for each record in the customer_orders table.
### D. Pricing and Ratings
- Calculate revenue based on fixed pizza prices and additional charges for extras.
- Design and implement a ratings system for customer orders.
---
## Conclusion
After analyzing the data and answering the case study questions, Pizza Runner can gain valuable insights to improve its operations and customer satisfaction:
- By understanding pizza metrics, runner experiences, and ingredient optimization, Pizza Runner can streamline operations and improve service quality.
- Implementing a ratings system allows Pizza Runner to gather feedback and improve customer satisfaction.
- With data-driven decisions, Pizza Runner can grow, become more profitable, and build customer loyalty in the competitive food delivery market.
